Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 18, 2026

Introduction

Pickup ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Check-ins: When you check in at a basketball court, we store an anonymous identifier associated with your device
  • Photos: If you upload photos of courts, we store those images
  • Location: We access your device location to show nearby courts (only when the app is in use)

Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, app version
  • Usage Data: Courts you view, check-ins, and app interactions
  • Location Data: Approximate location based on GPS (when permitted)

How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Show you basketball courts near your location
  • Display real-time check-in activity at courts
  • Provide weather information for courts
  • Allow you to save favorite courts
  • Improve our services and user experience

Data Storage and Security

  • Anonymous Users: We do not require account creation. Users are identified by an anonymous device ID
  • Check-ins: Automatically expire after 3 hours
  • Photos: Stored securely on Supabase cloud storage
  • Location: Not stored permanently; used only to calculate distance to courts
  • Security: We use industry-standard encryption and security measures

Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request information about what data we have
  • Delete: Request deletion of your data (check-ins expire automatically after 3 hours)
  • Opt-Out: Disable location permissions in your device settings
  • Photos: Delete photos you've uploaded through the app

Children's Privacy

Our app is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us.

Location Data

  • Permission: We request location permission to show nearby courts
  • Usage: Location is used only when the app is active
  • Storage: We do not store your precise location
  • Control: You can disable location access in device settings

Data Retention

  • Check-ins: Automatically deleted after 3 hours
  • Photos: Retained until deleted by uploader or moderators
  • Anonymous IDs: Retained until app is uninstalled

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the "Last Updated" date and displaying a notice in the app.

California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights:

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of sale of personal information (we do not sell data)

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email above.

European Users (GDPR)

If you are in the European Economic Area:

  • Legal Basis: Legitimate interest in providing our services
  • Rights: Access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability
  • Contact: support@pickupbasketball.app

Summary

We collect minimal data (anonymous IDs, location for nearby courts, optional photos). We don't sell your data. Check-ins expire automatically after 3 hours. You control location permissions. Contact us with questions.
